# Claim-to-Evidence Lock Before Publication

## Rule

Do not treat a manuscript as submission-ready until each major claim is tied to explicit supporting evidence.

## Checklist

- each claim has a traceable metric, figure, or source
- negative results are not hidden
- unsupported superiority claims are removed
- limitations are explicitly stated
- human-facing summary matches the actual benchmark record

## Why it matters

This protects against narrative drift between experimentation and publication.
