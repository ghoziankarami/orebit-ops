# Example - Offshore Tin Exploration Question

## Question

What exploration sequence makes sense for early-stage offshore tin evaluation when budget is limited and subsurface certainty is still low?

## Context

The goal is to reduce uncertainty in a staged way rather than overcommitting to expensive drilling too early.

## Initial Answer

A practical sequence is often:
1. compile existing geological and bathymetric context
2. review historical production and placer behavior
3. define target zones and uncertainty map
4. use lower-cost survey methods first where possible
5. escalate into drilling only where the expected information gain is meaningful

## Unknowns
- local seabed conditions
- sampling density already available
- vessel and contractor constraints

## Follow-up Research Needed
- compare survey options vs drilling cost
- define what decision threshold justifies next-stage drilling
