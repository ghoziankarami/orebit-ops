# Manuscript and Workflow Lessons

## Legacy workflow value

The older project had a useful separation between:
- active research workspace
- human-facing final output hub
- transitional or generated support folders

## What to preserve

- manuscript workflow discipline
- evidence mapping
- final review surfaces for humans
- explicit method notes and SOPs

## What not to preserve blindly

- raw scratch directories
- generated support clutter
- oversized intermediate artifacts
- runtime-specific folder sprawl

## New-vault interpretation

In the current vault, the equivalent pattern is:
- active research work -> `1. Projects/Research Programs/`
- durable synthesis -> `3. Resources/Research Notes/`
- reusable method -> `3. Resources/Frameworks/` or `3. Resources/Operating Systems/`
