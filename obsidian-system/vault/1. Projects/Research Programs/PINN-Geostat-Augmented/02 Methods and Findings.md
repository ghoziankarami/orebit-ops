# Methods and Findings

## Operating style

The reviewed legacy documents show an evidence-first iterative benchmarking process.

## Documented pattern

- Round 1: spatial-only baseline
- Round 2: geology-aware feature engineering with ensemble models

## Important result pattern

- spatial-only baseline outperformed naive geology integration
- engineered geology-aware features improved performance
- ExtraTrees was documented as the best reported model in the benchmark path reviewed

## Method lessons

- feature quality matters more than feature count
- reproducibility matters
- negative results are useful research outputs
- do not claim superiority over untested methods
