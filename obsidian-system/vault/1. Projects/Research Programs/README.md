# Research Programs

Use this lane for active or legacy-significant research packages that still deserve project-level structure.

## Good fit

- ongoing research programs
- research lineages that need multiple notes
- paper or manuscript programs
- investigation programs that may promote insights into durable resource lanes

## Pattern

A good research package often includes:
- overview
- research summary
- methods and findings
- workflow or manuscript lessons
- source lineage
- promotion tracker
